Support Zellij - Get Stickers

Support Open Source Development

Zellij is developed and maintained full-time by Aram Drevekenin, with no other source of income. The project will always remain:

  • Free and Open Source: Available to everyone, forever
  • Ad-Free: No advertisements or tracking
  • Privacy-Respecting: Your data stays yours
  • Community-Driven: Built for and with the community

Get Zellij Stickers

As a thank you for supporting the project, recurring monthly sponsors receive exclusive Zellij stickers! These high-quality stickers are perfect for laptops, water bottles, or anywhere you want to show your love for terminal workspaces.

How to Get Stickers

  1. Become a monthly sponsor with a recurring donation of $5-20/month
  2. You’ll receive stickers and a hand-written note as a thank you gift
  3. Continue supporting the development of new features and maintenance

Why Sponsor?

Your sponsorship directly enables:

  • Full-time Development: Dedicated attention to features, bugs, and improvements
  • Rapid Response: Quick fixes for critical issues
  • New Features: Implementation of requested functionality
  • Documentation: Comprehensive guides and tutorials
  • Community Support: Active engagement with users

Every contribution, no matter the size, makes a real difference in keeping Zellij development sustainable.

Other Ways to Support

If recurring sponsorship isn’t possible right now, you can still help:

  • Star the Repository: Show your support on GitHub
  • Share Zellij: Tell colleagues and friends about the project
  • Contribute Code: Submit pull requests and improvements
  • Report Bugs: Help make Zellij better for everyone
  • Write Tutorials: Share your workflows and use cases
